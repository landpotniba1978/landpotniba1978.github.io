'In Silence' is the eighth single by Japanese rock band Luna Sea, released on July 15, 1996. It reached number 2 on the Oricon singles chart.[1] The song's promotional video features a young Eiji Wentz. The song was used as the theme song for season 1 of the Japanese dub of the American television drama Chicago Hope.[2]
